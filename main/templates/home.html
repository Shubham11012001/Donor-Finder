Why Donate Blood?

Hospitals require stores of donor blood every day because they need to use it to keep people alive while treating injuries or performing surgeries. When there is a shortage, it can cause hospitals to stop performing procedures until they can get more. That is why it is important for hospitals to have a constant supply. Blood does have a shelf life, so it is impossible to store supplies for long periods of time. Regular donations can help and it can be easy if you keep a few tips in mind.
